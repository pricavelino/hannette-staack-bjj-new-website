About the Champion

More Than a Fighter.
A Movement.

Hannette Staack is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt, co-founder of Brazil-021 School of Jiu-Jitsu, and one of the greatest competitors in the sport's history. With a total of eight world titles and an ADCC championship, her legacy on the mats is unmatched.

Beyond her achievements, Hannette is recognized as an exceptional teacher and a key figure in strengthening the female presence in Jiu-Jitsu — inspiring a new generation of athletes around the world.

8× Jiu-Jitsu World Champion (GI & NO-GI) 3× ADCC Champion IBJJF Hall of Fame Inductee Professional MMA Fighter (3–1–0) BJJ Black Belt 5th Degree Brazil-021 Co-Founder
